THE THEORY OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


Module 1: THE FUNDAMENTALS OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


Objectives

1. Describe the field of Information Technology
2. Outline the history of Information Technology
3. Distinguish among data, information and knowledge


In today’s digital world, Information Technology (IT) is at the core of nearly every industry and aspect of our daily lives. Whether it’s the devices we use, the software that powers them, or the networks connecting them, IT is essential to how we work, communicate, and learn. This video below by "Of The Internet Things" will guide you through the foundational concepts of IT, including hardware, software, networking, and information systems.
